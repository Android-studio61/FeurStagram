# Code of Conduct

FeurStagram is a small open-source project. The goal is to keep discussion useful, respectful, and focused on making the project safer and easier to use.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith, but keep technical claims specific and verifiable.
- Stay on topic in issues, pull requests, and community spaces.
- Report bugs with enough context for someone else to reproduce them.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Posting malicious files, phishing links, or unsafe installation instructions.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who make the project unsafe or unproductive.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer privately if a private channel is available, or open a minimal issue asking for one.
